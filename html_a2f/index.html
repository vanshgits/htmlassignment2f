About us

MADE RIGHT. MADE DELICIOUS.
MADE ESPECIALLY FOR YOU.

The story of canada's fastest growing pizza place started back in 2011 when tarang bhatia opened his very first pizzabuddy in his hometown Calgery. Inspired by his own search for delicious pizza options, he started to wonder if there was a better way of doing pizza and business. The last thing the world needed was another pizzabuddy, but maybe this one could be different and everyone could get exactly what they wanted, made fresh on demand. It all started sounding pretty great, and soon tarang was opening the first pizzabuddy in his hometown and there was no looking back. As of today, Pizza Buddy has 500+ outlets across Canada with the recent one opening in London.

The secret to our food? It is pretty simple. Source fresh, quality ingredients. Prepare dishes with care and passion. Serve them with a warm smile. In other words, we take pride in what we make and how we share it. Even though we have grown a lot over the years, our mission is still the same: Always the best food, made especially for you
